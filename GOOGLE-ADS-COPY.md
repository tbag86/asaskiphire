# ASA Skips - Google Ads Copy

## Google Ads Best Practices for Generating Calls

**Research Summary:**
- Include price points to qualify leads
- Use local area names for relevance
- Add urgency with "Same Day" or "Today"
- Include trust signals (Licensed, Insured)
- Use action verbs (Call, Book, Get)
- Mention specific services/sizes
- Keep headlines punchy and benefit-focused

## Campaign Tips

1. **Set up Call Tracking** - Google Ads can track calls from the ad
2. **Mobile Bid Adjustment** - Increase bids for mobile (higher call intent)
3. **Schedule Ads** - Run during business hours (7am-8pm) for best response
4. **Local Targeting** - 15-mile radius around Hyde
5. **Negative Keywords** - Add: free, DIY, hire purchase (only relevant searches)
