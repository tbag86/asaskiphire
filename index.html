Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our skip hire services

What size skip do I need?

The skip size you need depends on the amount of waste you have. For a small bathroom renovation or garden clearance, a 4-yard skip is usually sufficient. For larger projects like a full house clearance or kitchen renovation, you might need a 6-8 yard skip. Our team can provide expert advice to help you choose the right size for your project. Give us a call on 07828 131272 and we'll guide you.

How long can I keep the skip?

Our standard skip hire includes up to 1 week rental. This gives you plenty of time to complete your project at your own pace. If you need the skip for longer, just let us know and we can arrange an extension for a small additional fee. We're flexible and will work around your schedule.

Do I need a permit to place a skip on the road?

If you're placing the skip on your own private property (driveway, front garden, etc.), you don't need a permit. However, if the skip needs to be placed on the public road or pavement, you'll need a permit from your local council. We can help advise you on this and assist with the permit application process if needed.

What can't I put in the skip?

We accept most types of household and commercial waste, including wood, metal, plastics, garden waste, and general building materials. However, we cannot accept hazardous materials such as asbestos, chemicals, paint, tyres, electrical items (WEEE), fluorescent tubes, or fridges/freezers. If you're unsure about any items, please give us a call and we'll be happy to advise.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Subject to availability, we can often provide same-day skip delivery across Tameside and surrounding areas. If you need a skip urgently, call us on 07828 131272 before 10am and we'll do our best to get one to you the same day. We also offer next-day delivery as standard.

What happens to my waste?

We're committed to environmental responsibility. Once we collect your skip, we take it to a licensed waste transfer station where it's sorted. We recycle as much waste as possible - typically over 80% of general waste can be recycled. Only materials that cannot be recycled are disposed of safely and legally in accordance with environmental regulations.

How do I book a skip?

Booking a skip is easy! Simply call us on 07828 131272 or fill in the contact form on this page. We'll ask you a few questions about your requirements (size needed, delivery location, dates) and provide you with an instant quote. Once you're happy, we'll arrange delivery at a time that suits you.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all of Tameside including Hyde, Ashton-under-Lyne, Stalybridge, Mossley, Dukinfield, Denton, Droylsden, and Audenshaw. We also serve surrounding areas including parts of Manchester, Stockport, Oldham, and Glossop. If you're not sure if we cover your area, give us a call and we'll let you know.
